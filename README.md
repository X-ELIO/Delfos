# Delfos

**Delfos** is an AI engine designed to bridge the gap between organizational strategy and individual performance — helping employees define personal objectives that are meaningfully aligned with company goals.

---

## What is Delfos?

Organizations often struggle to translate high-level strategic objectives into concrete, day-to-day goals for each employee. Misalignment leads to wasted effort, disengaged teams, and strategies that never fully materialize.

Delfos solves this by acting as an intelligent advisor: it understands where the company is going, where each individual stands, and guides the goal-setting conversation to produce objectives that are both personally motivating and strategically relevant.

The name draws from the Oracle of Delphi — the ancient source of clarity and direction. Delfos aspires to be that same source of clarity within modern organizations.

---

## Core Capabilities

- **Company goal ingestion** — Delfos reads and understands the organization's strategic objectives, OKRs, or any structured goal framework.
- **Individual context awareness** — It considers each employee's role, seniority, team, and prior performance to provide personalized guidance.
- **Objective co-creation** — Through a conversational interface, Delfos helps employees draft individual objectives that connect to the broader strategy.
- **Alignment scoring** — Each proposed objective is evaluated for its degree of alignment with company priorities, giving managers and employees a clear signal before committing.
- **Iterative refinement** — Delfos suggests edits, reframes vague goals into measurable ones, and flags objectives that lack strategic relevance.

---

## How It Works

```
Company OKRs / Strategic Goals
         │
         ▼
   ┌─────────────┐
   │   Delfos    │  ◄──  Employee profile, role, team context
   │  AI Engine  │
   └─────────────┘
         │
         ▼
  Individual Objectives
  (aligned, measurable, motivating)
```

1. **Input** — The organization's goals are loaded into Delfos (via API, document upload, or integration with HR/OKR platforms).
2. **Profile** — Each employee's context (role, team, level) is provided so Delfos can tailor its recommendations.
3. **Conversation** — The employee interacts with Delfos to explore, draft, and refine their objectives.
4. **Output** — A final set of individual objectives with alignment rationale, ready for manager review and approval.

---

## Why Delfos?

| Without Delfos | With Delfos |
|---|---|
| Objectives set in isolation from company strategy | Objectives explicitly linked to strategic priorities |
| Managers manually check alignment across dozens of reports | Alignment is scored and surfaced automatically |
| Goal-setting feels like a bureaucratic exercise | Goal-setting becomes a guided, meaningful conversation |
| Strategic drift goes undetected until review cycles | Misalignment is caught early, before work begins |

## Target Users

- **Employees** — looking for clarity on how their work connects to the bigger picture.
- **Managers** — who need to ensure team objectives are coherent and strategically sound.
- **HR / People teams** — running performance cycles and OKR processes at scale.
- **Leadership** — seeking visibility into how strategy is cascading across the organization.

## Status

This project is currently in the **proof-of-concept** stage. The goal is to validate the core alignment loop — from company objective ingestion to individual goal generation — before moving toward a production-ready system.
